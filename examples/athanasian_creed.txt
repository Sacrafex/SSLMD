@AthanasiusOfAlexandria #Creed

=Whoever= *Desires* !ToBeSaved! >+Must+ *HoldCatholicFaith*  
&IF&*NotKeepWholeAndUndefiled*=true> >!PerishEternally!

=ThisIs= *CatholicFaith* >!Worship! +One God+ /In Trinity/ +Trinity/ /In Unity/

&NOT&=ConfoundingPersons= &NOT&=DividingSubstance=

=Father= +Uncreated+ =Son= +Uncreated+ =HolySpirit= +Uncreated+  
=Father= +Infinite+ =Son= +Infinite+ =HolySpirit= +Infinite+  
=Father= +Eternal+ =Son= +Eternal+ =HolySpirit= +Eternal+  

&YET&!NotThreeEternals! >+OneEternal+  
&NOT&=ThreeGods= >+OneGod+  
&NOT&=ThreeLords= >+OneLord+

@Father =Made= +Neither By+ =None=  
@Son =Begotten Of= +Father Alone+  
@HolySpirit =Proceeds From= +Father And Son+

>!NoneBeforeAnother! >!NoneGreater! >!NoneLess!

=InThisTrinity= >+All Co-Eternal+ +All Co-Equal+  
>+Unity In Trinity+ /Trinity In Unity/

=Therefore= *MustBelieve* >!Trinity In True Godhead!

&FURTHER&=NecessityOfFaith=  
=Lord Jesus Christ= >!Son of God+And+Son of Man!

@HeIsGod= +Of+ =Substance Of Father=  
@HeIsMan= +Of+ =Substance Of Mother=  

>!Perfect God! >!Perfect Man!  
+Reasonable Soul+ +Human Flesh+

>!EqualTo Father! =AsToDivinity=  
>!LessThan Father! =AsToHumanity=

&THOUGH&=HeIsGodAndMan= >!NotTwoButOneChrist!

=One= *Not By* =Conversion Of Godhead Into Flesh=  
=One= *By* =Assumption Of Humanity Into God=

>!One Altogether! *Not By Confusion Of Substance*  
>But By *Unity Of Person*

@Jesus =Suffered= /For Salvation/  
+Descended Into Hell+ >!Rose Again! /Third Day/  
+Ascended Into Heaven+  
>Sits @RightHandOfFather  
>From There >!WillCome To Judge! /Living/ /Dead/

=At His Coming= >!All Shall Rise! /With Their Bodies/  
+Render Account For Their Deeds+

&THEN&=ThoseWhoHaveDoneGood= >!EternalLife!  
&THOSE&=WhoHaveDoneEvil= >!EternalFire!

=This Is= *Catholic Faith*  
&IF&*NotBelieveFaithFullyAndFirmly*=true> >!CannotBeSaved!

ENDALL
